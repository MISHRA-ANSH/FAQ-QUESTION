Netflix FAQ
What is Netflix? +
Netflix is a streaming service that offers a variety of TV shows, movies, and more on thousands of devices.
How much does Netflix cost? +
Netflix offers different plans ranging from Basic to Premium. Visit the website for pricing details.
Can I watch Netflix offline? +
Yes, Netflix allows downloading of select titles to watch offline on mobile devices.
What devices can I use? +
Netflix works on Smart TVs, consoles, phones, tablets, and more.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Yes, you can cancel anytime via the Account page.
